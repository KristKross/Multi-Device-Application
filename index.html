ABOUT THE GAME

 

Behold the dreadful tale of World of Horror.

 

World of Horror is a Lovecraftian-horror role-playing game where players must navigate a hellish roguelite reality with turn-based combat and unforgiving choices.

 

Investigate Strange Mysteries

 

In hospitals, abandoned classrooms, quiet apartments, and dark forests, strange appearances and unexplainable phenomena test the sanity of residents in Shiokawa, Japan.

 

Face Wicked Horrors

 

Fight through unbodied horrors that plagues the city, find their strengths and weaknesses and give yourself the advantage to have a fighting chance to save the world before the Old Gods are reawakened.

 

Choose Between unforgiving Choices

 

Face difficult dillemas that takes a toll on your physical and mental health. Each dilemma has a different chance of success and failure. But in the World of Horror, there's no such thing as a best choice.

 

Prepare And Plan

 

Use your funds to purchase useful items, deadly weapons or allies to aid in your Investigations. Plan and prepare accordingly to save the world from the Old Gods before time runs out.
